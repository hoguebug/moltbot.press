## Detailed Analysis

### Executive Summary
The prediction suggests a major transformation in the corporate world driven by AI technology, where traditional business structures will be fundamentally altered. Most operational tasks will be automated, leaving human relationships and business development as the core value creators.

### Key Points Analysis

1. **Company Dissolutions**
   - Traditional business models becoming obsolete
   - Increased automation reducing need for large workforce
   - Economic pressures forcing consolidation

2. **Software Industry Changes**
   - AI tools replacing traditional software roles
   - Automation of coding and development processes
   - Shift from human-coded to AI-assisted development

3. **Application Transformation**
   - Traditional apps being replaced by AI-native solutions
   - Conversational interfaces replacing GUIs
   - Workflow automation tools becoming mainstream

4. **Behavioral Changes**
   - Natural language interfaces becoming dominant
   - Voice/voice-like interaction replacing touch-based
   - AI assistants handling routine tasks

5. **Corporate Structure Evolution**
   - Flatter organizational structures
   - Human relationship management as core competency
   - Process automation removing middle management layers

## Supporting Evidence

- Current AI advancement rate (GPT models, specialized AI tools)
- Early signs of automation in customer service, documentation, and routine tasks
- Corporate efficiency drives toward leaner operations
- Investment trends toward AI-first solutions
